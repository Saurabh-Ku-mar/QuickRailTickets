Common questions

Everything you need to know

Still have questions? Chat with us on WhatsApp →

How long does ticket confirmation take?

Usually under 5 minutes during business hours (8 AM – 10 PM). After you submit the form and complete the UPI payment, send us the payment screenshot on WhatsApp and we'll confirm your ticket right away.

Which trains and classes are available?

We book tickets on all major Indian Railway trains including Rajdhani, Shatabdi, Duronto, Mail/Express, and Passenger trains. All classes — Sleeper, 3AC, 2AC, 1AC, and CC — are available.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept UPI payments via any app (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, etc.). Scan the QR code or enter our UPI ID directly. Net banking and card payments coming soon.

Can I book tickets for someone else?

Yes! Just enter the traveller's name in the form and your own mobile number for WhatsApp communication. We'll send the ticket to your WhatsApp.

What is the cancellation and refund policy?

Cancellations follow Indian Railway's standard cancellation charges. Contact us on WhatsApp with your booking reference and we'll process the cancellation. Refunds are credited within 5–7 working days.

Is my payment information safe?

Yes. We use UPI — a system regulated by the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI). We never ask for your bank credentials, OTPs, or card details. Your money goes directly to our verified UPI ID.
